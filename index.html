Simple Menu Effect as seen on YouTube

Inspired by the left side menu found on YouTube/NOT ANYMORE. When clicking on the menu label and icon, the main menu appears beneath and the menu icon slides to the right side while the label slides up. To close the menu, the menu icon needs to be clicked again.
